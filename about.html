Few Words About us

Re-cycle cycling club is one of the most recognized and friendly club in Norway founded in 2010. Our club composed of more than 150 male and female cyclists who participates in most of the cycling activities. Our club is very consious about safety of clients even childrens also. We appoint kids loving and patient staff to take care of kids during the tour. Our club comes together with social events and offer guided tours to the city, mountains, near the lakes to have a beautiful view of the town. We also arrange a major whole city tour in summer time with discounted rates.
